The Dawoodi Bohra Community uses the Kanz Al Marjaan fonts to write text and make books for their community.

The Latin glyphs are derived from Source Sans 3 by Adobe, and the Arabic ayah, rub-el-hizb and sajdah symbols from Amiri by the Amiri Project Authors, both used under the SIL Open Font License.

To contribute, see https://github.com/HatimMasvi/Kanz-al-Marjaan.
